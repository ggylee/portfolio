Hi, I’m Gill, an aspiring Web Developer with a background in recruitment, most recently as part of in-house teams at tech companies for five years.

As an effective communicator and team player adept at juggling priorities, I’m now looking for opportunities that will help me hone my technical skills. Ultimately, I would love to build products and solutions that have a positive impact on peoples’ lives.
